TITLE	Solution structure of the ectodomain of the human SidT1 glycoprotein   
BIOISIS_ID	SIDT1P
EXP_DESCRIPTION	Previous biochemical and electron microscopy studies of the extracellular domain of putative dsRNA transporter SIDT1 suggested the protein formed a higher order oligomer. However, the particle's relatively small size for EM (~175 kDa) and lack of large, distinctive features prohibited further structural studies aimed at obtaining a 3D reconstruction. We therefore sought to better understand the protein's assembly and shape using SAXS.
