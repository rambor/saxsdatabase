TITLE	Human Regulator of Chromosome Condensation (RCC1) 
BIOISIS_ID	HRCC1P
EXP_DESCRIPTION	RCC1 is a chromatin-associated guanine nucleotide exchange factor (GEF) for the small GTPase Ran. RCC1 and Ran play key roles in nucleo-cytosolic protein transport and nuclear envelope assembly during mitosis. RCC1 is a WD40 domain containing protein and also a homolog of plant UVR8. Our SAXS data highlight the biological monomer of RCC1.
