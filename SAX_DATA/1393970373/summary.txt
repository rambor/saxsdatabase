TITLE	MnmE bound to GDP-AlFx
BIOISIS_ID	MNME2P
EXP_DESCRIPTION	SAXS data of E. coli MnmE bound to GDP-AlFx. MnmE was expressed in E. coli and purified via Ni-NTA affinity chromatography followed by the removal of bound nucleotides using alkaline phosphatase. Subsequently MnmE was purified via a second Ni-NTA affinity chromatography and gel filtration. MnmE was measured in the presence of 1 mM GDP-AlFx. Sample homogeneity was assesed using SEC-MALS and DLS.
SOURCE_LOCATION	X33 DESY,  Hamburg Germany
