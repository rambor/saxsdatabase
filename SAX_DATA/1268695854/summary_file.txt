TITLE	The Structure and Conformation of Lys63-Linked Tetra-Ubiquitin(Ub)
DESCRIPTION	Since the crystal structure of the extended conformation of the Lys63 tetra - Ubiquitin is clearly influenced by lattice contacts, we used small-angle X-ray scattering combined with molecular dynamics simulations to explore the structure of Lys63 tetra-Ub in solution. Small-angle X-ray scattering experiments show that the tetra-Ub chain is dynamic, adopting an ensemble of conformations that are more compact than the extended form in the crystal. The results of these studies provide a basis for understanding the differences in the behavior and recognition of Lys63 poly-Ub chains.
