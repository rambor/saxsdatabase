TITLE	Ca-dependent conformation of N. caninum CDPK1

BIOISIS_ID	

EXP_DESCRIPTION	Apicomplexan CDPKs (Calcium Dependent Protein Kinase) undergo radical conformational rearrangement in response to changes in calcium concentration. This deposition contains SAXS curves measured for N. caninum CDPK1 in buffer differing by the presence of either 1 mM CaCl2 or 1 mM EGTA.  These can be compared to the scattering predicted from crystal structures of Ca-bound and Ca-free homologs.
