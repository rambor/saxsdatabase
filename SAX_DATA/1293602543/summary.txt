TITLE	Solution Structure of SAM-1 Riboswitch bound
BIOISIS_ID	2SAMRR
EXP_DESCRIPTION	The SAM-1 riboswitch is a metabolite sensing RNA aptamer. Crystallographic studies of the bound and unbound states of the riboswitch suggest nearly identical structures whereas SAXS studies suggest differing conformations in solution. This dataset represent SAXS data of the SAM-1 riboswitch collected in the presence of SAM and is explained well by the X-ray crystal structure (PDB: 2GIS).
SOURCE_LOCATION	ALS BL 12.3.1
PUBLICATION	Free state conformational sampling of the SAM-I riboswitch aptamer domain. Structure. 2010 Jul 14;18(7):787-97.
